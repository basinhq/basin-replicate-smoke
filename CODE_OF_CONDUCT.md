# Code of conduct

## Our standards

We want participation in this project to be respectful, constructive, and
welcoming.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- showing empathy and respect for differing experiences and viewpoints
- giving and accepting useful technical feedback
- focusing discussion on what is best for the project and its users
- taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, personal
attacks, sexualized language or imagery, deliberate disruption, and publishing
someone else's private information without permission.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing
the project or its community.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the repository maintainers. Reports
will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical. Project
maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.

Maintainers who receive a report involving themselves must recuse themselves
from its review.
